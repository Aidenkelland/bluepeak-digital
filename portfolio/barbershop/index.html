Our Story

Built on craft, not shortcuts

The Fade Barbershop started with one chair and a simple idea: a haircut should be an experience, not an errand. Every barber on our floor trains for months before they touch a paying client, and every tool gets cleaned between every cut.

We keep the shop small on purpose — fewer chairs, more attention, and a standard we don't compromise on.
