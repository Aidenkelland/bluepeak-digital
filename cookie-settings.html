Cookie Settings

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This page explains exactly what runs on bluepeakdigitalhq.com and why there's no cookie consent banner: it's because this site doesn't set any tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

1. What this site doesn't do

We don't use Google Analytics, ad pixels, or any third-party tracking or advertising cookies. Nothing on this site profiles you, follows you across other websites, or sells your data to advertisers.

2. What actually runs on this site

  • Theme preference (light/dark): held only in your browser's memory for the current visit. It isn't written to a cookie or local storage, and resets the next time you load the page.
  • Contact form (Formspree): when you submit the "Get my free quote" form, your submission is sent to Formspree, a third-party form-handling service, so we can receive your enquiry. Formspree's own practices — including any cookies it may set as part of processing your submission — are covered in Formspree's privacy policy, not this one.

Neither of these requires cookie consent under most privacy laws, since they're either not cookies at all, or are strictly necessary to provide a feature you directly requested (sending us your enquiry).

3. If that ever changes

If we later add something like privacy-friendly analytics, we'll update this page first and add a proper consent mechanism before anything non-essential is set — not after. Check the "last updated" date above if you want to confirm nothing has changed since your last visit.

4. Related policy

For how we handle the personal information you do choose to give us (like your name and email on the contact form), see our Privacy Policy.

5. Contact us

Questions about cookies or tracking on this site? Email bluepeakdigitalweb@gmail.com or WhatsApp us.
