Our Story

Roasted slow, served warm

Ember started as a single roaster and six tables. These days we still roast in small batches, source direct from farms we've actually visited, and keep the menu short so everything on it is done properly.

Come for the coffee, stay for the corner table by the window — it's usually free before 8am.
